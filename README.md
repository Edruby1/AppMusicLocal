# LocalMusic

Hello! 👋

LocalMusic is a simple music player for **Android** that plays your local MP3 files. This project was built with love during my free time with the goal of providing a lightweight, reliable, ad-free, and completely free music player.

## Features

- 📱 Android only
- 🎵 Play local MP3 files
- 📂 Organize your music library
- 🚫 No ads
- 💯 Completely free

## How to add music

1. Download an MP3 file to your Android device.
2. Open the app and tap the **+** button.
3. Select your music file and grant the required permissions.
4. Enjoy your music!

> **Note**
>
> LocalMusic does **not** copy your music files into the app. It only stores a reference to their location. If you delete, move, or rename the original file, it will no longer be playable from the app.
